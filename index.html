❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes — COD is available across Pakistan. You pay when your order arrives at your doorstep.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders reach you in 3–5 working days after confirmation, depending on your city and custom work.

Can I order a fully custom design?

Absolutely. Send your idea or reference photo on WhatsApp. We share a mockup before we start crafting.

Are two pieces exactly the same?

No — every item is handmade, so each piece is one of a kind. That is the beauty of Resin Dreams.

How do I place an order?

Tap any WhatsApp button on this site, tell us what you want, and we guide you through size, colour, and payment.
